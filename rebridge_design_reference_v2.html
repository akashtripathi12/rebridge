The premium-retail system

Restyled toward warm premium retail — Nike's photography-first editorial discipline (near-monochrome chrome, towering display type, colour reserved for price signal) fused with Apple's warm pearl surfaces and edge-to-edge product staging. This page is the spec. Companion: rebridge_ui_v2.html (grading reveal + Health Card, fully animated).

04 · 3D + assets

The dark stage is for the real product

Every dark stage in this system is a mount point. In the hero and the grading reveal, replace the SVG stand-in with the Three.js GLB scanner: orbit the real sneaker, amber scan sweep (TSL shader), defect hotspots pinned on the mesh. Higgsfield fills the rest.

3D — Product Scanner

r3f + drei. Dark studio, amber rim light, contact shadow. GLB sneaker (Higgsfield 3D) with procedural fallback. OrbitControls idle auto-rotate; on Grade → TSL emissive band sweeps up the mesh → hotspots pop → grade + confidence. WebGPU renderer, WebGL fallback.

Higgsfield 1 — hero loop

Macro slow-motion of a worn white & orange sneaker rotating on a dark turntable, thin amber laser scan line sweeping across revealing glowing inspection markers, fine dust in rim light, deep charcoal background, photoreal, seamless loop, 4K

Higgsfield 2 — demo opener

Cinematic dusk aerial: a glowing parcel travels a 600 km highway on a dark map of India, trail fades red; cut to the same parcel taking a 4 km path glowing green to a warm apartment window, minimal, premium, slow push

Rules: video/3D is the hero of the dark stages, never decoration elsewhere. Loop < 8s, < 6 MB. If an asset isn't ready, the SVG/procedural fallback ships — the layout never breaks.
